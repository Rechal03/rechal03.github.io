About

I'm a Full-Stack Engineer and AI systems developer currently completing my M.S. in Computer Science at Texas Tech University. My work spans two connected worlds: building reliable backend systems and APIs, and building the retrieval and reasoning layers that make LLMs actually useful in production.

Recently I re-architected a production RAG pipeline — chunking, embedding, and re-ranking — to improve retrieval precision while cutting response latency under real query load. I also built ResearchGPT from scratch: a document-grounded question-answering system running entirely on local, open-source models.

I like problems where the correctness of an answer matters — where "the model made something up" isn't an acceptable failure mode.
